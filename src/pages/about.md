I'm Hamidreza. I've spent the last decade or so working on machine learning
in one form or another — across a few industries, a few countries, and a
lot of different problems. This site is where I write when I want to think
something through more carefully than a conversation allows.

The posts here tend to gather around a few recurring interests: the ideas
behind modern ML, how we evaluate the systems we build, and the stubborn
non-linear structure of most things worth studying. Some of it is technical.
Some of it is just me trying to reason out loud.
